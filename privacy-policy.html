1. Service Warranty

Harsha Technologies stands by the quality of its repairs. We offer:

  • 30-Day Service Warranty: On the specific issue fixed by our technician. If the same problem reoccurs within 30 days, we will fix it free of labor charges.
  • Spare Parts Warranty: Replacement parts carry a warranty as per the manufacturer (usually 3-12 months). Physical damage or electrical surges void this warranty.

2. Doorstep Service Policy

Our technicians visit your home or office for inspection and repair.

  • A minimum visiting/inspection charge applies if you decide not to proceed with the repair after diagnosis.
